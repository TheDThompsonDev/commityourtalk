## ğŸŒŸ What is Commit Your Talk?

Commit Your Talk is a structured learning platform that helps software engineers:
- Overcome fear of public speaking
- Practice in a safe, supportive environment
- Progress from 30-second intros to conference talks
- Build careers through better communication
- Join a community of developer speakers

## ğŸ¯ Learning Paths

### 1. Speaking Foundations (Beginner - 6 weeks)
Break out of your shell with supportive exercises. Learn structure, manage nerves, and deliver your first tech talk.

### 2. The Conference Speaker (Intermediate - 8 weeks)
Craft compelling talks, design great slides, and write CFPs that get accepted.

### 3. Technical Deep Dive (Advanced - 6 weeks)
Master explaining complex technical topics clearly. Live coding, architecture diagrams, and expert Q&A.

Develop executive presence and strategic communication skills for technical leaders.
